How to use our webapp

Step by step explanation

  • Step-1

    Register and Login

    First the user needs to register and then login.

  • Step-2

    Slots

    There are two slots available one for amazon and one for flipcart, choose one.

  • Step-3

    Choose your slot and upload

    Choose either amazon slot or flipcart,capture a picture of the box and then there will be a upload button option, click on that. with the help of our backend we do the verification process and if verification was successful you will be displayed a success message, given a point and an email will be sent.

  • Final Step

    Pickup details

    Once the verification is successful you will be shown a form, enter the pickup details, once done the boxes will be collected.

  • And
    that's it !
